Our story

Built by refugees, for refugees

Founded in 2015 by members of Lancaster's growing Bhutanese-Nepali community, BCD Lancaster is a place families can turn to when everything is new. Many of us arrived as refugees ourselves — so we understand the road, and we walk it with each new family.

What we do

Support at every step

Practical, hands-on help for the moments that matter most.

🏠

Newcomer & Resettlement Support

Help getting settled — housing, essential documents, benefits, and the basics of daily life in a new home.

🧭

Community Integration

Guidance navigating schools, jobs, transportation, and civic life so families can participate fully and confidently.

💚

Health & Wellbeing

Connecting families to healthcare and raising mental-health awareness — a priority for our community's younger and older members alike.

👪

Youth & Family Programs

Programs that engage young people, strengthen families, and keep our culture and language alive across generations.

Everyone deserves the chance to belong.

Our mission is to help Bhutanese-Nepali refugees in Lancaster County resettle with dignity, integrate into the wider community, and build stable, thriving futures.
